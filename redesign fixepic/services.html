COLOR CORRECTION

Color Correction is the method of changing the colors present in a picture. This could be a distinct change (one color to other) or an unpretentious change (white balance). Also, the techniques like Brightness and contrast correction, white balance adjusting, correcting the exposure, straightening the photo, sharpening and adjusting the noise, lighting adjustment etc, are employed to get the desired result. Please provide the artist your specific requirements to edit your image.
